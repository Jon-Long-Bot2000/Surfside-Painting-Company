FAQs

Please reach us at support@vargasconstructionus.com if you have any additional questions.

What features are included in a Vargas Construction ADU?

Each of our ADUs are fully equipped with modern amenities tailored to your needs, including a full kitchen, spacious bathroom, comfortable living area with heating and AC, and convenient washer/dryer hookups.

Can I rent my ADU?

Absolutely!  In California, housing affordability is a major issue. Not only will you be able to gain rental income but you can help others in finding affordable housing.

How long is the process?

Depending on the size of your project, and the amount of time it takes for the permit approval, the construction timeline can take anywhere from 2-6 months.

Can I customize the design of my ADU?

Absolutely! We work closely with you to design an ADU that fits your specific style and space requirements, ensuring your new unit feels like a perfect extension of your home.

Are Vargas ADUs built to be energy-efficient?

Yes, we prioritize sustainability. Our ADUs are constructed using energy-efficient materials and appliances to reduce environmental impact and save you money on utilities.
